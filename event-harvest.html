Fall Harvest Festival

Join us as we celebrate the end of the growing season with a joyful community gathering filled with food, music, and fall-themed activities. This is our way of honoring the hard work poured into the garden all year long — and sharing the abundance with everyone.

From fresh produce tastings to pumpkin painting and local vendor booths, this festival is open to all ages and brings neighbors together in gratitude and celebration.

Our Goal

The Fall Harvest Festival brings our community full circle — from planting to harvesting, and finally, to celebration. It's a reminder that the garden doesn’t just feed us; it connects us. Through this event, we share food, traditions, and joy while deepening our roots with one another.

  • Fresh produce stands with garden-grown veggies
  • Live music and local performers
  • Pumpkin decorating & face painting for kids
  • Seasonal recipe tastings and food demos
  • Garden tours and fall planting info
  • Community gratitude wall & photo booth
